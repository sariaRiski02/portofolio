About Me

Hello! I'm Rizky Saria, an enthusiastic informatics engineering student born in September 2002. I am currently honing my skills in web development, driven by a strong belief in the potential of the web to solve many problems efficiently.
The web's ability to operate across all platforms without the need for multiple app installations makes it incredibly versatile. Imagine the inconvenience if every service required its own app—this would consume significant memory space on our devices. The web, however, offers a seamless, efficient solution.
While I advocate for web solutions, I also recognize that advanced web applications can sometimes benefit from being developed into mobile applications for enhanced functionality and user experience.
Feel free to explore my portfolio and projects. If you have any questions or collaboration ideas, don't hesitate to reach out!
